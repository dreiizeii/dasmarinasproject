City of Dasmariñas

About

Kolehiyo ng Lungsod ng Dasmariñas (KLD) is a public higher education institution located in Dasmariñas City, Cavite, committed to providing quality, accessible, and community-oriented education. The college offers a variety of undergraduate programs that aim to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for professional excellence and social responsibility. With modern facilities, dedicated faculty, and a focus on holistic development, KLD serves as a hub for learning, research, and civic engagement, contributing to the growth and progress of both its students and the wider community.


Date of Establishment: 2023
