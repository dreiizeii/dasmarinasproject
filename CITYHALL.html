Dasmariñas Socio-Economic Building

City of Dasmariñas

About

The Dasmariñas Socio-Economic and Multi-Purpose Building is a modern six-story government facility that serves as the new City Hall of Dasmariñas City, Cavite. It was designed to centralize government offices and public services in one location, providing efficient, accessible, and citizen-friendly service delivery. With features such as a penthouse, garden deck, helipad, and integrated public spaces, the building symbolizes the city’s commitment to progress, innovation, and sustainable urban development.


Date of Establishment: 2023

Floor Area: 22,774.75 sqm
