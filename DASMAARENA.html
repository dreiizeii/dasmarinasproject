Dasmariñas Socio-Economic Building

City of Dasmariñas

About

The Dasmariñas Arena is a modern multi-purpose facility in Dasmariñas City, Cavite, designed to host athletic competitions, cultural events, and large public gatherings. With its contemporary design and functional layout, the arena provides a safe, organized, and accessible space for residents and visitors, promoting sports development, recreation, and community engagement. Serving as a central hub for local and regional events, the Dasmariñas Arena reflects the city’s commitment to active lifestyles, youth development, civic pride, and continuous progress.


Date of Establishment: 2023

Floor Area: 11,415 sqm

Capacity: 5,000 People
